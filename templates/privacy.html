Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes information you provide to us directly and information we get from your use of our services.

Personal Information

  • Contact details (Name, Email, Phone Number)
  • Account credentials and security information
  • Communications you send to us

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate our platform, such as IP addresses, browser types, and operating systems.

How We Use Your Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract with you, and compliance with our legal obligations.

Primary Uses

  1. To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  2. To send administrative information to you.
  3. To fulfill and manage your requests or orders.
  4. To protect our Services and ensure security.

Information Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

Your trust is our priority. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their promotional purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process.

End-to-End Encryption

Data is encrypted both at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols.

Secure Infrastructure

Our servers are hosted in SOC 2 compliant data centers with 24/7 monitoring.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request copies of your personal data.

Right to Rectification

You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.

Right to Erasure

You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
